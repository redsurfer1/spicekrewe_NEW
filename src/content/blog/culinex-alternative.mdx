Small brands often need something narrower: **a clean-label reformulation**, **one hero SKU** documented for scale, or **a sensory target** translated into vendor-ready language. Paying for open-ended access when you needed **forty focused hours** is how scope quietly expands—and how finance starts asking why culinary spend looks like another full-time salary.
