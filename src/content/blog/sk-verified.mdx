# What is an SK Verified culinary professional? How to vet a freelance chef before hiring

**SK Verified** is Spice Krewe’s credentialing lane for culinary professionals on our marketplace. It signals that a talent profile cleared portfolio review, reference checks, rate validation, and fit-for-scope review—not that the platform replaces food-safety licensing or legal liability for your event.

Buyers use the badge to shortlist faster; talent uses it to signal serious commercial readiness. [Browse SK Verified culinary professionals](/talent?verified=true).

## What SK Verified means

1. **Portfolio review** — documented work aligned to claimed specialties (CPG, styling, private dining, and so on).
2. **Reference checks** — commercial or chef-to-chef validation where available.
3. **Test or trial engagement** — selectively used when scope is novel or high stakes.
4. **Rate validation** — published rates are reviewed for marketplace realism (SK anchors include Marcus Johnson **$175/hr**, Aisha Thompson **$165/hr**, Dana Nguyen **$190/hr**, Rafael Cruz **$150/hr**).
5. **Badge assignment** — only after the above gates; revoked if quality signals fall below Krewe standard.

## Why vetting matters for food brands

Food brands carry **liability**, **brand voice**, and **regulatory** exposure. Unvetted freelance boards optimize for low bid—not documentation discipline. SK Verified narrows the field before your legal or procurement team sees the shortlist.

## What SK Verified does not mean

It is **not** a government license, **not** insurance, and **not** a guarantee of outcome. It **is** a structured review you can trust more than a five-star average on a generic gig site.

## How to check SK Verified status

Open the talent directory and filter to verified profiles, or look for the gold **SK Verified** badge on individual profiles. Start here: [browse SK Verified culinary professionals](/talent?verified=true).

## Frequently asked questions

### What is a vetted culinary professional marketplace?

A marketplace that reviews credentials and work samples before professionals appear in buyer search—Spice Krewe does this through **SK Verified** gating.

### Is there a culinary freelancer platform with vetting?

Yes—Spice Krewe combines SK Verified review with brief intelligence and AI-assisted matching so vetting happens **before** you negotiate scope.

### What questions should I ask before hiring a food consultant?

Ask for **deliverable format**, **revision rounds**, **IP ownership**, **timeline**, **who owns grocery or lab spend**, and **how they document handoff to your co-packer or kitchen**.
