# How AI matches food brands with chefs — how culinary talent matching works online

Hiring a chef on an open marketplace often means skimming **dozens of unvetted profiles** with conflicting rates and unclear deliverables. Spice Krewe narrows the field with **SK Verified** talent first, then uses **Gemini-assisted matching** against a structured brief—not keyword spam alone.

For credential context, read [what is an SK Verified culinary professional](/blog/sk-verified)—this post focuses on the **matching mechanics**.

## How the Gemini matchmaker works

### Step 1 — Client posts a brief

You describe cuisine, dietary needs, deliverables, timeline, and budget band in plain language.

### Step 2 — Auto-Scoper generates a TRD

The **Auto-Scoper** drafts a **Technical Requirements Document (TRD)**—a production-ready scope in food terms. A minimal TRD snippet might look like:

```
Project: Clean-label dip line refresh
Cuisine spec: Southern + Creole accents, dairy-forward baseline
Dietary: gluten-free claim path, no artificial colors
Deliverables: 3 bench formulas, nutritional handoff memo, photo-ready hero batch
Timeline: 6 weeks to pilot co-packer date
Budget band: $150–$190/hr SK Verified R&D window
```

### Step 3 — Match against SK Verified profiles

Gemini scores the TRD against profile tags, past project types, certifications, **$150–$190/hr** rate bands, and availability signals.

### Step 4 — Ranked matches within about 48 hours

You receive ranked professionals with portfolio context—not an unfiltered directory dump.

## What makes a match “good”?

Depth on **cuisine**, proof on **CPG vs event** work, **dietary** experience, and **SK Verified** status all lift confidence. Thin profiles rarely survive ranking when the TRD is specific.

## What clients should expect (and what they should not)

Expect **transparent scope** and **ranked shortlists**. Do not expect the model to replace **your** legal, safety, or insurance decisions—those stay with your brand.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I vet a freelance chef before hiring?

Use **SK Verified** profiles, ask for **deliverable samples**, and align on **IP + revision rounds** in writing. See also [what is an SK Verified culinary professional](/blog/sk-verified).

### What questions should I ask a food consultant?

Clarify **handoff format** (docs, spreadsheets, bench photos), **who attends plant trials**, and **how pricing moves if scope expands**.

### Does AI replace human judgment?

No—it **accelerates shortlisting**. You still choose the professional and approve the contract path.
